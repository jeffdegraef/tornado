How to buy a Raspberry Pi

What do I need to use TheThingBox ?

  • A raspberry Pi2 (better)
  • A Power supply : 5V 1A micro USB power supply to power your Raspberry Pi (to know if your adapter is enough check this link )
  • A SD card : 8 Gb is the minimum
  • An ethernet (RJ45) cable to connect TheThingBox to the network
  • Optional WiFi dongle

Notice that you will also need a usual computer to connect you to the ThingBox.

Where can I buy a raspberry-pi ?

You can find a raspberry-pi and all accessories on e-shops like Amazon (search for 'raspberry pi').

Or on : https://www.raspberrypi.org/products/raspberry-pi-2-model-b/
